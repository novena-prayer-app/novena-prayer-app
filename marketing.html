The Heart-Check
Why Nós

Built like a chapel, not a dashboard.

Most prayer apps reward consistency like a productivity system. Nós was designed to feel slower, warmer, and more honest. It is a companion for sacred time, not a tool for self-optimization.

Quiet Design

Stillness first.

Prayer screens are stripped of noise and built for reading slowly. Serif typography, warm paper tones, and gentle pacing make the app feel handmade and reverent.

Honest Prayer

No guilt loops.

Nós does not shame missed days or turn devotion into streak anxiety. The experience stays patient, grounded, and faithful to the actual rhythm of prayer.

Whole Story

Room for every answer.

After Day 9, the Heart-Check acknowledges three realities: answered prayer, waiting, and peace in the no. That distinction is central to the product, not an afterthought.

How It Works

A 9-day rhythm that stays personal.

The flow is simple by design: choose a novena, name a brief intention, pray day by day, and reflect honestly on what happened. The app supports the practice without crowding it.

1

Choose a Novena

Browse a calm library of Catholic novenas organized by patron and situation.

2

Name Your Intention

Capture the heart of your prayer in just a few words, the way you would carry it into a chapel.

3

Pray Each Day

Read the day’s prayer, additional prayers, and reflection in an immersive format built for attention.

4

Complete the Heart-Check

Mark the prayer answered, still waiting, or no, then continue into testimony, patient waiting, or peace.

The Heart-Check

The part most prayer apps ignore.

After the ninth day, Nós does not assume triumph. It asks what happened, then meets the answer with reverence instead of simplification.

Answered

Give thanks without spectacle.

Testimony is treated like something earned. Gold accents mark the light breaking through, while the journal keeps the tone grounded and personal.

Waiting

Stay with the prayer.

Some novenas become longer stories. The waiting path keeps the cycle present without turning uncertainty into failure.

Peace in the No

Honor the hard answer.

Nós treats no as a valid and dignified spiritual reality. The app provides a quiet space for acceptance, not a dead end.

Privacy

Your prayer life stays yours.

Intentions, journal entries, and prayer progress are treated with care. The product is designed to be intimate, not extractive.

No ads

No ad networks, no social feed pressure, and no attention hacks layered onto prayer.

User-scoped data

Prayer data is stored securely and separated by user, with privacy built into the backend model.

Offline-friendly reading

Core prayer content is cached for quiet use even when connection is limited.

Designed For

People who want a quieter way to pray.

Whether you are starting your first novena or returning to a long-loved devotion, Nós is built to feel gentle, reverent, and clear. It supports practice without trying to dominate it.
