Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Your prayer life is private. We built Novena with that in mind.

Who We Are

Novena is a Catholic prayer app created by Carter Shannon ("we," "us," or "our"). By using Novena, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you have questions, contact us at novena.app.customer.service@gmail.com.

What We Collect

We collect only what we need to make Novena work for you:

  • Your name and email address when you create an account
  • Prayer intentions, novena progress, journal entries, and testimonies you create in the app
  • Your situation preferences and onboarding survey responses
  • Your notification settings and time preferences
  • Anonymous, aggregated usage data to improve the app (no personal identifiers)
A note on prayer intentions: Your prayer intentions and journal entries may reflect personal religious beliefs. We treat this information with the highest level of care. It is stored securely, never shared, never sold, and never used for advertising.

How We Use Your Information

  • To run the app and keep your prayer data synced across sessions
  • To suggest novenas that match your situation or intentions
  • To send daily prayer reminders, only if you enable them
  • To display the anonymous global prayer count (no personal data included)
  • To respond to your support requests

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Supabase, a secure cloud database provider. We use row-level security — only you can access your own data. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

Prayer content is also cached locally on your device so you can pray offline. This local cache contains only prayer texts and reflections — no personal data.

We Will Never Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising, marketing, or any commercial purpose.

Third-Party Services

Novena uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Authentication and data storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Sign-In — Optional sign-in method (Privacy Policy)
  • Sign in with Apple — Optional sign-in method, coming soon (Privacy Policy)

We do not use any advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or data brokers.

Your Rights

  • Access your data at any time within the app
  • Update your name and preferences in Settings
  • Delete your account and all associated data from Settings › Delete Account
  • Request a copy of your data by emailing us
  • Turn off notifications at any time in Settings or your device settings

Account deletion is permanent and immediate. All your data — intentions, journal entries, novena progress, and testimonies — is deleted from our servers. This cannot be undone.

California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at novena.app.customer.service@gmail.com.

Children

Novena is rated 4+ on the App Store and contains no mature content. However, our account-based features are intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page and post a notice in the app. Continued use of Novena after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
